INTERESTS



Besides the obvious interest in technology which is easily gauged by how over-engineered this website is, below are some other interests.

Meditation

I've had a daily meditation practice since 2012 and have been guiding meditation groups for three years. If you live in Western Mass and are interested in meditation, checkout our group which is a local chapter of the Institute for Applied Meditation. I also have training and experience with Centering Prayer. I was a resident volunteer at the Lama Foundation in 2014 where I served as winter retreat coordinator and maintainer of the off-the-grid solar system.




Chess

I've always enjoyed a casual game of chess. From time to time I work on my chess game and have steadily improved to about a 1650 UCI rating. I like to play unconventional openings often discovered with my own chess app. I'm definitely a positional player and a kind of artist archetype when it comes to chess—a good game is a thing of beauty. Some of my favorite players are Aron Nimzowitsch, José Capablanca, and Judit Polgár (I especially recommend her children's books).



Sailing

I take particular interest in sailing in honor of my grandfather and father's naval service. I initially learned to sail in Puerto Rico as a teenager and began my ASA certification in Lake Travis near Austin. I'm excited to be out of the deserts of New Mexico and closer the ocean!





Science Fiction / Fantasy

Do you refer to multiple Star Trek episodes by their official names such as 'The Best of Both Worlds?' When you hear the word Taiga do you think of a dual land? Do you know that Lin means melody in High Elvish? Do you know why Mace Windu had a purple lightsaber? Welcome to my world ;)
